## ingredients

1. produce
	- [ ] 4 peppers
	- [ ] 2 onions
	- [ ] garlic
	- [ ] lime
	- [ ] avocado
2. meat
	- [ ] 1.33lb sirloin; topRound, flank
3. carb
	- [ ] pita
4. seasoning
	- [ ] 1/2 cup beef broth
	- [ ] fajita seasoning

## instructions

- [ ] medium chop peppers and onions
- [ ] thin cut the steak
- [ ] smash a few garlic
- [ ] beef stock
- [ ] half of fajita seasoning packet
- [ ] 5 min pressure-cook
- [ ] 10 min low release
- [ ] squeeze in lime
- [ ] serve on pita
- [ ] top with avocado

## improvements

- reduce cook time, pressure / release
- cut steak thinner
- double recipe
